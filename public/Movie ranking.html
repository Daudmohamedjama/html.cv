The best movies according to Daud

My top three movies

1-Inception (2010) – Sci-Fi/Thriller

A mind-bending thriller directed by Christopher Nolan about a group of thieves who infiltrate dreams to steal secrets. It’s intense, clever, and visually stunning.

2-The Social Network (2010) – Drama/Biography

Directed by David Fincher, it tells the story of the founding of Facebook and the legal battles that followed. It’s smart, fast-paced, and features great performances.

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse (2018) – Animation/Action

A fresh, visually dazzling take on Spider-Man with multiple versions of the character from different dimensions. It’s fun, unique, and emotionally compelling.
